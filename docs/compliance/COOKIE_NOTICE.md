## Overview

ConsolidaSuite uses only strictly necessary functional cookies to provide authentication and session management. We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or any third-party cookies.

---

## Cookies We Use

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Type | Flags | Duration |
|------------|---------|------|-------|----------|
| `access_token` | JWT authentication token used to verify the identity of the logged-in user on each request | First-party, functional | `HttpOnly`, `Secure`, `SameSite=Strict` | 15 minutes |
| `refresh_token` | Used to obtain a new access token when the current one expires, maintaining the user's session | First-party, functional | `HttpOnly`, `Secure`, `SameSite=Strict` | 7 days |

### Cookie Attributes Explained

- **HttpOnly:** The cookie cannot be accessed by JavaScript running in the browser, protecting against cross-site scripting (XSS) attacks.
- **Secure:** The cookie is only transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.
- **SameSite=Strict:** The cookie is not sent with cross-site requests, protecting against cross-site request forgery (CSRF) attacks.

## Consent

The cookies used by ConsolidaSuite are **strictly necessary** for the functioning of the service. Under Article 5(3) of the ePrivacy Directive (Directive 2002/58/EC), strictly necessary cookies are exempt from the requirement to obtain user consent.

Because we only use strictly necessary cookies, ConsolidaSuite does not display a cookie consent banner. No consent is collected, stored, or managed for cookies.

---

## How to Disable Cookies

You can configure your browser to block or delete cookies. Instructions vary by browser:

- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
- **Edge:** Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies

**Important:** If you disable cookies for ConsolidaSuite, you will be unable to log in or maintain an authenticated session. The authentication cookies are required for the service to function.
